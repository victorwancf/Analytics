Stock Price Prediction with K-nearest Neighbors (KNN) and Random Forest

Predicting stock price is one of the popular fields where machine learning is applicable.
Market trends, news, price action, and performance of the industry are some of the key factors affecting the stock price. This project will focus on the price action and aims to predict stock price on the next day whether it is up or down, utilizing KNN, random forest, and financial indicators to maximize the accuracy of the model.
